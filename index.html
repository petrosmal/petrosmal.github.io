My Projects

San Francisco Salaries

This project employs SQL to analyze San Francisco city employee salaries from 2011 to 2014. It aims to uncover trends in compensation distribution, including base pay, overtime, and benefits across various employee groups. Additionally, the analysis seeks to identify any evidence of gender-based pay discrimination within the dataset. Ultimately, this exploration will provide insights into budget allocation and workforce dynamics within the city government.

Climate Change

In this project, I use data from ClimateData API and various machine learning models to predict the temperature change over the next decade. The models are trained on historical climate data, including temperature, precipitation, and CO2 levels, to forecast future climate trends. The project aims to provide insights into the potential impact of climate change on our environment and help in planning mitigation strategies.

Medical Appointment No-Shows

This project focuses on analyzing medical appointment data to predict no-shows. By using various machine learning algorithms and data analysis techniques, I aim to identify the factors that contribute to patients missing their appointments. The insights gained from this analysis can help healthcare providers improve their appointment scheduling and reduce no-show rates, ultimately enhancing patient care.
