My Projects

San Fransisco Salaries

This project employs SQL to analyze San Francisco city employee salaries from 2011 to 2014. It aims to uncover trends in compensation distribution, including base pay, overtime, and benefits across various employee groups. Additionally, the analysis seeks to identify any evidence of gender-based pay discrimination within the dataset. Ultimately, this exploration will provide insights into budget allocation and workforce dynamics within the city government.
